Image Design Projects

I dabble in a few different areas of design. Here are some of the projects that I've worked on most recently.

Into the Ocean book Stick season book Remembered Portraits

This was a project based on the song Into the Ocean by Blue October.I wanted to theme it after the ocean, but I wanted to add a twist. So, instead of using imagery from the ocean, instead I tried to parallel the ocean to the seas of grass that we have here in Colorado where I grew up. I also wanted to skirt around the color blue, so I used purple and green instead, to allude to the blue of the ocean without using the color outright.

These portraits I did for a movie that I wrote and co-directed a couple years ago called Remember(ed) We took stills of all of our characters and our crew from set and I drew these stylized portraits for our credits. A special thanks to our cast and crew for being such wonderful models!
